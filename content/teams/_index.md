## Cost per Player

Final cost depends on format and tournament chosen. R3VA covers all team-level expenses (tournament entry, refs, field rental, insurance allocation, coaching admin). Per-player rates include a small operational margin (~15%) for coaching and program sustainability.

| Format | Tournament | Roster Size | Per Player |
|---|---|---|---|
| **7v7** | NC (July) | 12–15 | **$215** |
| **8v8** | Richmond (June) | 14–17 | **$265** |
| **10v10** | NC (July) | 16–18 | **$200** |
| **10v10** | Richmond (June) | 16–24 | **$282** |

*Source: R3VA 2026 Internal Cost Breakdown. Larger rosters spread fixed costs (tournament entry, field rental) across more players.*

## What's Included

- ✔ All practices at River City Sportsplex (4 or 6 sessions)
- ✔ Tournament entry fee
- ✔ Insurance / USA Lacrosse compliance
- ✔ Coaching at all practices and tournament games
- ✔ Pinnie/uniform

**Not included:** Personal gear (stick, helmet, gloves, pads, cleats, mouthguard). Gear specifics are shared with confirmed players after teams form.

## Payment Options (once teams form)

- **Full payment** at registration, OR
- **2-installment plan** — 50% at registration, 50% auto-charged 2 weeks later. Auto-debit, cannot opt out.

## Refund Policy

- **Within 21 days of registration:** Full refund including processing fees.
- **After 21 days:** Stripe's processing fee (~3%) is non-refundable.
- **If team doesn't form** (insufficient enrollment): full refund of all fees.
